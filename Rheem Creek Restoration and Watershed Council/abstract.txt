
Abstract:
The Rheem Creek Restoration and Watershed Council project will complete the following: 1.  Establish a Rheem Creek Watershed Council.2.  Restore 700 linear feet of Rheem Creek at Bayview Elementary School.3.  Restore 1,200 linear feet of Rheem Creek at Contra Costa Community College and the Rollingwood      neighborhood. 4.  Develop an education program integrating restoration, revegetation, monitoring and water     conservation activities with the West Contra Costa Unified School District (WCCUSD), Contra Costa     College (CCC) science department, and local residents.
